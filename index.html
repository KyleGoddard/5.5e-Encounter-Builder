Schema reference

Required: name, size, type, alignment, ac, hp (object with average), cr, six abilities (str, dex, con, int, wis, cha). Recommended: xp, speed, initiative, proficiency_bonus, ability modifiers/saves (e.g. str_mod, str_save), senses, languages, and arrays for traits, actions, bonus_actions, reactions, legendary_actions.
